Warehousing

Harlink Investment Ltd stores substances in its warehouses for customs clearance and risk control until it has a written release authorization from the exporter and a twin telecellsmartphone backup, detailing mode and issue of release. To take advantage of this service, the interested party writes to Harlink Investment Ltd asking for this service and provides it with the necessary information regarding the nature and types/portions of items, size/numbers of packing containers, and names of the party to the transaction. Harlink Investment Ltd (HIL) imports materials into its warehouses for storage in its completed items warehouse. It also has a NAFDAC approved chemical warehouse where it stores items other than highly inflammable items. It does not accept drums of highly inflammable materials into its chemical warehouse, or any item that is highly flammable. There are also open areas in which chemicals are stored in drums.
