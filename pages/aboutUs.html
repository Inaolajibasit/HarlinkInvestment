Harlink Investment

The Company:

HARLINK INVESTMENT Ltd (HIL) was conceived and structured in line with global maritime regulations as a local international logistics company. The desire of the founding partner was to nurture a wholly family company into a multinational player in the global logistics value chain. Harlink is prepared to compete and partner with foreign companies that are desirous to do business in Nigeria's maritime space. The company was registered with the Federal government and has increased the share capital from 1M to 20M fully paid. This enables Harlink to be registered with federal regulatory agencies in the marine and oil & gas sectors of the economy.

LICENSES AND PERMITS
